My Story

My name is Natalia Kukula. I am originally from Poland and I have been living in Chicago for over 2 years. I decided to become a web developer to satisfy my need for problem solving and helping others. My educational background is in public relations and media marketing and has led me to the hospitality startup industry and corporate advertising, which has helped me gain high level communication skills and managerial expertise. As a globtrotter, I developed entrepreneurial interest towards human-centered design, which came from my need of finding "pretty things" that inspired creativity durning my travels.
